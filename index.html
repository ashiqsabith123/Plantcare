Welcome to plant care



Plant care is a farming related website. From this website farmers and others can ask doubts about farming related topics and admin can reply to those doubts. Also farmers can sell their products (vegetables or fruits etc) on this website. Both farmers and other peoples can login to this website for purchase. This website is for establishment of an effective system for protection of plants varities the rights of farmers and plants breeders, and to encourage the development of new varites of plants and to protect the rights of farmers. This website allow user to sign up and enter a complaint or register their needs.
